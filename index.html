// 01 — About

Who I am

I'm Andrew Tang, a networking and cloud engineering student passionate about understanding how data moves through modern infrastructure — from the Data Link layer all the way up to distributed cloud services.

My hands-on labs span network security, multi-protocol routing (OSPF, EIGRP, BGP, IS-IS), AWS cloud fundamentals, and local AI deployment. I'm CCNA certified and constantly building on that foundation.

Outside of the terminal, I'm interested in the intersection of privacy-first technology and practical deployment — whether that's locking down a Layer 2 network or running an LLM entirely on local hardware.
